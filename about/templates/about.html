All about MyBookYourBook

MyBookYourBook.com is thé website for those who love to read! We offer anyone the opportunity to buy and sell books ánd to use our rent-a-book-system. This way more people can enjoy more books.

Our Goal

We want as many people as possible to be able to read as many books as possible. When we read, not only are we improving memory and empathy, research has shown that it makes us feel better and more positive too. Now, why wouldn't we want that for everybody?

How do we work?

Anyone can buy, sell, rent and rent-out books. All you have to do is register. If you want to sell a book, simply log in, fill in the form, and you're good to go. If you want to buy a book, you can simply browse through all the books on offer, or you can use our searchbar to find the book you're looking for.

Buy a book

Buying books is really simple on MyBookYourBook.com. When you find a book you like, simply click the buy button and you see a form all filled in for you to send to the provider of the book. Of course you can change the message, or you can just hit the send button to send an email to the provider. It's up to you and the provider to arrange the deal.

Sell a book

When another user wants to buy a book you have on offer, you will recieve an email to arrange things. When a book is sold, please remove it from MyBookYourBook.com.

Rent a book

Renting makes it possible to read a book for a nice price ánd makes it possible for the owner of the book to earn some money without having to sell the book. How it works? If you want to be able te rent a book, just check the 'I want to be able to rent(-out) books'-checkbox when you register or when you update your profile. You will then be asked to make a 30 euro deposit. This will be used to pay the rent. Now, when you find the book you want to rent, you add it to your shoppingcart and when you're done, you can let us now by sending us the content of the shopping cart through out form. We'll take care of the rest. When you return the book to the owner, simply reply to the message we send you and ask te owner to do the same. We'll take care of the finances and let you know your balance.

Rent-out a book

If you want to be able te rent-out a book, just check the 'I want to be able to rent(-out) books'-checkbox when you register or when you update your profile. You will then be asked to make a 30 euro deposit. Any money you make from renting out books will be added to your deposit. When another user wants to rent one of your books, you will get an email from us to arrange things. When a book is rented out, please remove it from MyBookYourBook.com.

Deposit

When you want your deposit back, or want us to pay you the money you earned by renting out books, just send us an email, with your account number, through our contact-form.
